# Correlation buckets (Layer 2)

Positions on the same underlying theme win and lose together — three
contracts on the same election are one bet in three costumes. Instruments
carry a `correlation_bucket`; open risk within a bucket is capped at **6%**
of bankroll by default.

This is the constraint that catches the professional sports bettor's slate:
each individual bet sizes fine on Kelly, but the tenth bet on the same
Saturday card finds the bucket nearly full and gets downsized to the
remaining headroom.

Bucket assignment is honesty with yourself: if two positions share a "why",
they share a bucket.
